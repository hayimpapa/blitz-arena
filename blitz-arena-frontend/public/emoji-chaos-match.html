🎯 How to Play

Objective

Be the first player to collect 10 points by finding matching emoji pairs!

How to Play

  • Players take turns tapping two cards to reveal emojis
  • If the emojis match, they disappear and you earn a point
  • If they don't match, they flip back after 1 second
  • Remember where emojis are located to make matches!
  • Keep playing until someone reaches 10 points

Scoring

Each matching pair = 1 point
First to 10 points wins!
